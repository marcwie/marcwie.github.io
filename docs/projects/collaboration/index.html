My Collaboration Network

This is a visualization of my collaboration network. It is generated from the entries in my publication list . I have removed myself, i.e., the ego-node, as otherwise all co-authors would be connected to me and the network would become more star-like. Hover over the nodes to see which authors appear on the same papers. The width of the edges gives the frequency of collaborations between pairs of co-authors.
